TekProvider's Solutions

Connect with your customers at every stage of the lending process

TekConnect is the end-to-end solution for managing WhatsApp, SMS and email communications with your customers, as well as enabling OCR, proof of life, digital signature and biometric validation. It’s designed to integrate with any system on our platform. TekConnect improves operational efficiency and user experience with automated, real-time communication.

Functionalities

Keep your customers’ communication flowing.

WhatsApp integration

Automatically respond to customers and manage frictionless interactions through WhatsApp.

Notifications

Contact your customers by email, SMS and WhatsApp at the right time. The system can manage individual and bulk notifications.

OCR and document processing

Advanced tools for credit evaluation and delinquency management.

Life proof and biometric validation

Authenticate identities with facial recognition and advanced technology.

Integration with third parties through API

Easily connect with other systems to enhance communication and data validation.
