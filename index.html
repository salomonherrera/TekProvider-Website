Personal and Commercial Loans

We offer a flexible solution for institutions that provide financing to individuals or companies that need liquidity for various purposes: working capital, debt consolidation, home improvements, personal expenses, among others.

Our software automates the entire loan lifecycle, from origination and underwriting, to formalization, and contract and payment management, allowing institutions to operate with agility, minimize risks and offer a better experience to their customers.

Finance Lease

Our software supports institutions that offer asset and equipment finance leasing, facilitating the formalization of contracts where the client exercises the purchase option at the end of the term. We automate the entire process, from credit evaluation to contract management and payments, ensuring operational efficiency and regulatory compliance.

Operating Lease

Our solution is ideal for institutions that offer asset and equipment operating lease alternatives, where the client uses the asset with no obligation to purchase at the end of the contract. In addition to handling requests, validations, contracts and renewals, we have a robust asset management module that allows detailed control of leased equipment inventory, its location, contract validity, insurance, maintenance, and residual value, optimizing management and maximizing the return on assets.

Refinancing

We support institutions that provide asset and equipment refinance solutions, where companies sell an asset and subsequently lease it back, to generate cashflow and obtain liquidity.

Our system manages the operation securely, from contract structuring to payment tracking, minimizing risks and accelerating the process.

Factoring

Our platform facilitates the financing of accounts receivable through factoring, allowing institutions to originate, analyze and manage invoice assignment operations in an agile and secure manner. We automate from document reception to disbursement and collection, reducing time and operational errors.

Specific target credit

We offer a specialized solution for institutions that grant loans for a particular purpose, such as the acquisition of machinery, infrastructure, equipment or strategic projects. Our software controls from the validation of the credit destination to the management of disbursements and verifications, guaranteeing transparency and traceability.

Automotive Finance and Leasing

It’s a flexible and robust auto financing and leasing software solution.

Our platform manages the entire automotive financing and leasing process, from the reception of the application, validation of documentation, risk analysis, to the formalization, funding and follow-up of contracts.

Corporate Banking

Our solution is designed for institutions that serve companies of all sizes, from SMEs to large corporations. We facilitate the complete management of the credit and leasing cycle for businesses seeking financing for working capital, asset acquisition, lines of credit of all types, financial leases, among others.

In addition, we have a robust collateral management module, which allows us to register, value, monitor and release the collateral associated with each operation, ensuring precise risk control and strengthening the management of the corporate portfolio.
