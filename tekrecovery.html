TekProvider's Solutions

The ultimate solution for managing delinquent and default accounts

Maximize loan portfolio recovery with intelligent technology. TekRecovery is the ideal platform for finance companies to efficiently manage their collections, from early management to legal dispatch follow-up. It automates processes through workflows, streamlines account assignment and optimizes the effectiveness of your collections efforts.

Functionalities

Turn collections into a competitive advantage with TekRecovery.

Automation of the collection cycle

Control and segment preventive, administrative and legal collections from a single platform.

Third-party collection Agencies

TekRecovery can manage account collections through external collections agencies. It assigns, monitors, and evaluates the performance of the agencies in charge of delinquent and default recovery accounts.

Custom collection strategies

Design dynamic rules to contact customers at the most effective times.

Recovery reports and analysis

Get real-time metrics to optimize decision making and improve recovery rates.

Integration with financial systems

Connect with ERPs, CRMs and accounting systems for automated and efficient collections.
Gain competitive advantage with TekRecovery

Complementary modules

Strengthen your collections strategy with additional modules such as a mobile app for on-site management and zoning tools to efficiently segment and allocate receivables.

Mobile application for on the field collections

It allows debt collection field agents to make physical visits on debtor’s location, record evidence and update payment status in real time.

Geographic zone design module with Google Maps

Design strategic routes and assign accounts to agents/managers by geographic zones to optimize collection routes and management times.
